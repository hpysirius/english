### Lesson 77 Terible toothache
### 要命的牙痛

```
Listen to the tape then answer this question.
听录音，然后回答问题。

What time of day is it, do you think? How do you know?
你以为现在是几点钟？你怎么知道的？

Good morning. Mr. Croft.
早上好，克罗夫特先生。

Good morning, nurse.
早上好，护士。

I want ot see the dentist, please.
我想见牙科医生。

Do you have an appointment.
您预约好了吗？

No, I don't.
没有。

Is it urgent?
急吗？
urgent [ˈərjənt] 紧急

Yes, it is.
是的。

It's very urgent.
很急。

I fell awful.
我难受急了。

I have a terrible toothache.
牙痛得要命。

Can you come at 10 a.m. on Monday, April 24th?
您在4月24日星期一上午10点钟来行吗？

I must see the dentist now, nurse.
我必须现在就见到牙科医生，护士。

The dentist is very busy at the moment.
牙科医生这会儿很忙。

Can you come at 2 p.m.?
您下午两点钟来行吗？

That's very late.
那太晚了。

Can the dentist see me now?
牙科医生现在就不能给我看一下吗？

I'm afraid that he can't, Mr. Croft.
恐怕不能，克罗夫特先生。

Cna't you wait till this afternoon?
您就不能等到今天下午吗？

I can wait, but my toothache can't!
我倒是可以等。可我的牙痛等不了啊。
```
